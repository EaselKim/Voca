# Word Definition
1 abandon cease to support or look after; leave behind.
Example: She had to abandon her plans.
2 ability possession of the means or skill to do something.
Example: She has the ability to solve complex problems.
3 abrupt sudden and unexpected.
Example: His departure was abrupt.
4 absorb take in or soak up.
Example: The sponge can absorb a lot of water.
5 abstract existing in thought or as an idea but not having a physical or concrete existence.
Example: The concept was too abstract for me.
